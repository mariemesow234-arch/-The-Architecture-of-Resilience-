# Self-Regulation Techniques

## Grounding Methods
- *5-4-3-2-1 Sensory Awareness*: Notice 5 things you see, 4 things you feel...
- *Breath Anchoring*: Following natural breath rhythm
- *Body Scanning*: Progressive awareness from feet to head

## Cognitive Tools
- *Thought Labeling*: "I'm having the thought that..." instead of "I am..."
- *Perspective Shifting*: "How would I view this in one year?"
- *Metaphor Creation*: Transforming experiences through narrative

## Environmental Resourcing
- *Music Curation*: Building playlists for different emotional states
- *Space Arrangement*: Creating psychological safety in physical environments
