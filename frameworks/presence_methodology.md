# Presence Methodology Framework

## Core Principle
*Non-Judgmental Presence as Therapeutic Intervention*

### Fundamental Elements
1. *Silent Witnessing*
   - Creating psychological safety through attentive silence
   - Allowing emotional processing without interruption

2. *Reflective Mirroring*  
   - Validating emotional experience through careful paraphrasing
   - Helping individuals hear their own thoughts with clarity

3. *Cognitive Space Creation*
   - Using pauses to break automatic negative thought patterns
   - Creating room for new perspectives to emerge

## Implementation Guidelines
- *Posture*: Open, grounded, non-threatening
- *Language*: Simple, validating, non-interpretive  
- *Timing*: Following the other's natural rhythm
- *Boundaries*: Clear, consistent, respectful

## Ethical Framework
- Consent and autonomy as foundational
- Confidentiality and discretion
- Recognizing limits of peer support
- Knowing when professional help is needed
