# Theoretical Foundations

## Neuroscience Basis
- *Neuroplasticity*: Capacity for neural reorganization through experience
- *Mirror Neuron System*: Basis for empathy and emotional resonance
- *Amygdala-Prefrontal Dynamics*: Emotional regulation pathways

## Psychological Frameworks
- *Humanistic Psychology* (Carl Rogers): Unconditional positive regard
- *Attachment Theory*: Secure base for emotional exploration
- *Trauma-Informed Care*: Safety, trust, empowerment principles

## Key Researchers
- Bessel van der Kolk (trauma embodiment)
- Dan Siegel (interpersonal neurobiology)  
- Stephen Porges (polyvagal theory)
